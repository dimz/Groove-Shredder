The General Settings tab allows you to configure Groove Shredder's behavior. Automatic downloads do not require the user to click any buttons to download the song being played, and the other settings are self-explanatory.

The Download Settings tab contains the options related to saving a downloaded file to disk.

Experimental features may be unstable or may not work. If you use them, please report any bugs you find.

You may obtain advanced or specific help related to this addon at my blog. Please consider leaving a review. :)
